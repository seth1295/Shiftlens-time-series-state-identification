# Public Boundary

ShiftLens is a diagnostics and reproducible state-evidence toolkit for noisy time-series data.

ShiftLens produces diagnostics and reproducible state evidence only. It does not provide decision, execution, operational, or control authority.

Examples use toy or synthetic datasets unless otherwise documented.

Dependencies and third-party source attribution are documented through the standard project metadata, `NOTICE`, and `ATTRIBUTION.md`.

Generated reports are outputs for inspection and reproducibility. They are not authority for decisions or operations.
